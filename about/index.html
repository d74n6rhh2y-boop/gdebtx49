FAQ

How do you select games?
Quality, relevance, awards, signals. Every game personally tested and selected. Every game.
What sources are checked?
Official sites, the stores, communities, the awards. Cross-checked daily.
Where's the score?
There isn't one. Tastes and scores shift with every update and every new game. Being here is the score.
Why so concise?

They're labels. They're too broad. hexplay tags what you actually do in the game.
